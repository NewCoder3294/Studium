Step 1: Survey

Quickly skim the material to get an overview. Look at headings, subheadings, images, and summaries.

Step 2: Question

Turn headings and subheadings into questions. Write them down to guide your reading.

Step 3: Read

Read the material carefully, looking for answers to your questions.

Step 4: Recite

Summarize what you've read in your own words. This helps reinforce learning.

Step 5: Review

Go over your notes and questions to reinforce your understanding.
